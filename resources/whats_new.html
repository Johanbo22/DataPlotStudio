Welcome to DataPlotStudio

A data analytics and plotting tool all in one.

Latest Features:

  • NEW Statistical Tests:Select two numric columns in the Data Table, right-click and run one of three statistical tests (Pearson Correlation, T-Test and ANOVA). The results will be displayed in the new Test Results tab alongside an interpretation of the result.
  • NEW Regression analysis: Choose between Linear, Polynomial, Exponential or logarithmic models to create curves of fit based on your data.
  • NEW Export to Google Sheets: You can now push your dataset directly to Google Sheets using Service Account authentication. Provide your credentials as a JSON-file and upload your data to the cloud.
  • Data Cleaning Preview: The operations, "Remove Duplicates" and "Drop Missing Values", now highlight affected rows and requires a confirm to be removed.
  • Data Selection in plot: A selection tool to select points in a plot will redirect to the data explorer and highlight the selected points. Useful for pinpointing discrepancies in your data.
  • Color Blindness Mode: Apply colorblindness simulation to your plot to ensure correct coloring standards that adhere to general visualisation standards

Need help? Check the Help menu for documentation and tutorials.
